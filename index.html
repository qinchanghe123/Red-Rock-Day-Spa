FAQs

Before you arrive

A few quick answers to help you feel comfortable before your visit.

Please arrive 5-10 minutes early so you have time to check in, settle in and begin your session calmly.

If you have an injury, recent surgery or medical concern, please consult your healthcare provider first and let the spa know before your session.

Please call as soon as possible if you need to change or cancel your appointment so the time can be offered to another guest.

Parking is available near the spa location at 445 N Bluff Street in St. George.
